REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY
Last updated June 02, 2026

Thank you for subscribing to LaunchFly. This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work for your LaunchFly subscription. If you have any questions, email us at atomh25@gmail.com.

REFUNDS

We offer a 7-day refund window. If you are not satisfied with LaunchFly, you may request a full refund of your most recent subscription charge within 7 days of that charge. Refunds requested after the 7-day window are not guaranteed, but we may grant them at our discretion in cases of clear billing error or unintended renewal.

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To request a refund, email atomh25@gmail.com from the email address associated with your LaunchFly account. Please include:
  • The email on your LaunchFly account
  • The approximate date of the charge you'd like refunded
  • A brief reason (optional, but it helps us improve)
We review refund requests within 7 days of receipt. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days, depending on your card issuer.

CANCELLATIONS

You can cancel your subscription at any time. To cancel:
  1. Sign in to LaunchFly
  2. Open your account menu and choose Manage Subscription, which opens the Stripe Customer Portal
  3. Click Cancel subscription
After you cancel, your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period, after which it will not renew. We do not issue prorated refunds for unused time in the current period — if you cancel mid-period and want a refund for the remainder of the period, email us within the 7-day refund window described above.

RECURRING BILLING

LaunchFly is a subscription service that automatically renews each month at the price displayed at the time of purchase, until you cancel. Renewal charges are not eligible for refund outside the 7-day window described above, except where required by applicable law. You are responsible for managing your subscription — we recommend canceling before your next renewal date if you no longer wish to be charged.

EXCEPTIONS

We will issue a refund regardless of the 7-day window if:
  • You were charged due to a clear billing error on our part
  • The Service was materially unavailable for an extended period during your billing cycle and you were unable to use it
  • A refund is required by applicable consumer-protection law in your jurisdiction
